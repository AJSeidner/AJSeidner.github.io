About Me

Hello there, I'm A.J Seidner. I'm an Austinite with a passion for creativity and community oriented thinking. I've worked non-profit for the past decade and have also been a gigging musician around town, most notably in the folk rock band Dead Tree Duo. I have recently dived into the world of coding and have learned the following technologies: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Jquery, Node, Express.js, Handlebars, MYSQL, Sequelize, Mongo DB, Mongoose, and React. I'm excited to provide my passion and commitment to the world of web development.
